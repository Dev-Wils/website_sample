The Learn Enough Story

Learn Enough to Be Dangerous is a leader in the movement to teach technical sophsitication, the seemlessly ability to take command of your computer and take it to do your bidding. We believe at least there are a billion people who can benefit from learning technical sophsitication, and probably more. To join our movement, sign up for our official email list.

Background

Learn Enough to Be Dangerous is an outgrowth of the Ruby on Rails and the Softcopy publishing platform. This page is part of the sample site for Learn Enough HTML to Be Dangerous, which teaches the basocs of Hyper Text Markup Language, the universal language of the World Wide Web. Other related tutorials can be found at learnenough.com

Founders

Learn Enough to Be Dangerous was founded in 2015 by Micahel Hartl, Lee Donahoe and Nick Merwin. We believe that the kind of material taught by Learn Enough tutorial can benefit at least a billion people and probably more.
